# Lead Requirements Template

Use this template to collect lead generation requirements from the user.

## Target Criteria

### Industry & Market
- **Industries**: (e.g., SaaS, FinTech, Healthcare)
- **Company size**: (e.g., 10-50 employees, 50-200, 200+)
- **Geography**: (e.g., North America, EMEA, APAC)
- **Stage**: (e.g., Seed, Series A, Series B+, Public)

### Technical Profile
- **Tech stack**: (e.g., uses Python, AWS, React)
- **Job postings**: (e.g., hiring for "ML Engineer", "DevOps")
- **GitHub activity**: (e.g., active repos, specific languages)

### Engagement Signals
- **Recent funding**: (within last 6 months, 12 months)
- **Product launches**: (new features, major releases)
- **Team growth**: (hiring surge, expanding departments)
- **Social mentions**: (keywords, hashtags)

### Disqualifiers
- **Exclude industries**: (e.g., gambling, crypto)
- **Exclude companies**: (specific company names or domains)
- **Minimum requirements**: (e.g., must have raised $1M+)

## Output Preferences

- **Lead limit**: (max results per search)
- **Priority scoring**: (keywords/criteria that boost ranking)
- **Contact preferences**: (founder vs director-level, email vs LinkedIn)
